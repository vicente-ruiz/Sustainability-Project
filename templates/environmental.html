Environmental Impact of AI

Questions and Findings

Energy Consumption and Greenhouse Gas Emissions

What environmental costs are associated with the use of AI? The high computational requirements of AI-based systems lead to significant energy consumption, contributing to greenhouse gas emissions (8). Data centers, integral to AI operations, are expected to account for 14% of global emissions by 2040 (8).

E-waste and Environmental Challenges

How does AI contribute to e-waste and environmental challenges? The e-waste produced by AI technology poses a serious environmental challenge (8).
